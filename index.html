The Architect

In the liminal space between what is and what could be, Daniel 'Dnyaary' Carvalho exists as both creator and creation, architect and inhabitant of digital realms that challenge the boundaries of conventional reality.

Like the protagonists of Silent Hill navigating through fog-shrouded mysteries, or the operatives of Metal Gear Solid infiltrating impossible structures, Dnyaary crafts experiences that blur the line between the tangible and the ethereal.

Each project is a manifestation of the Control universe's impossible geometry—spaces that shouldn't exist but do, realities that bend to will and imagination. In this Death Stranding-esque landscape of connection and isolation, art becomes the bridge between worlds.

Welcome to the intersection of technology and consciousness, where every pixel is a portal and every interaction rewrites the rules of what digital experience can be.
